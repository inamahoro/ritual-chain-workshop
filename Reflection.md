# Reflection: Multi-Signature Verification in Bounties

Multi-signature verification decentralizes control by requiring multiple parties to approve the winner. This eliminates single points of failure and increases trust.

**Public vs Hidden:** Commitments are public. Approvals are transparent and traceable to individual signers.

**AI vs Human:** AI scores answer quality. Humans (signers) provide final approval, ensuring accountability.

**Advantages:** Decentralized control, no single point of failure, transparent approval process.

**Trade-off:** Requires coordination among signers, adds complexity, but increases trust.
